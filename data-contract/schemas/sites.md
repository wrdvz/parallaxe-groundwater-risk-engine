# `sites.parquet`

One row per site / establishment analyzable by the product.

## MVP columns

- `site_id`
- `siret`
- `siren`
- `site_name`
- `company_name`
- `address_line`
- `postal_code`
- `city`
- `lat`
- `lon`
- `is_geolocated`
- `is_icpe`
- `icpe_category`
- `naf_code`
- `naf_label`
- `source_url`
- `geo_score`
- `geo_type`
- `geoloc_confidence_label`
